Re-pulse
A game by Arthur Jones, Meara Kinne, Jill Gaynor, Stephen Cannavale, Jakob Okkels

Controls:

mouse-  look around

w-      move forward
a-      strafe left
s-      move backwards
d-      strafe right

e-      use switch
q-      toggle switch connection lines

shift + left click-   bind highlighted repulsor orb to left click (red)
shift + right click-  bind highlighted repulsor orb to right click (blue)

left click (hold)-    activate red repulsor orb
right click (hold)-   activate blue repulsor orb
